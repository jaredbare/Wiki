F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • My boyfriend/girlfriend broke up with me. How do I live?

    Realize that your life isn't over, even though it probably feels like it. What did you learn from the relationship? What can you do better for the next relationship to make it even more successful for the both of you? Remember it is OKAY if the relationship doesn't work- it doesn't have to be anyone's fault.

  • How do I stop myself from being nervous on the first date?

    Take a deep breath. Remember that this date is not a life-or-death situation- especially when it's a first date. Keep the basics in mind. The whole point is to be yourself, and see if you click with the other person. The first date is specifically for the two of you to get to know each other and enjoy each other's company. Nothing else!

  • Why is she ghosting me?

    First of all, just remember that this has happened to 99% of us. Most of the time, it isn't even personal, but here are some common reasons why: She's sensing you want more than her, and doens't want to deal with that. She likes someone else more, and doesn't want to lead you on. But most of the time, it's simply because they are too afraid to maturely communicate. Don't let it stress you out.

  • What are the best date ideas?

    There are two different kinds of dates: group dates and single dates. For group dates, go camping, go to a karaoke place, even a game night would be a blast. For single dates, you can go to waterfall hiking, ice skating, restaraunt hopping, and or even a simple move night.

  • When should I get married?

    Don't be discouraged by all the people around you that seem to be getting their life together, getting engaged, married, etc. Every person is different, and so is every relationship. Make choices that are right for yourself and your partner, and don't let others' input impact you too much.
